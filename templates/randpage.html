Complementary Colors: Opposite each other on the color wheel, these create high contrast, ideal for bold statements. Examples: red and green, blue and orange, yellow and purple.

Analogous Colors: Adjacent on the wheel, they create a harmonious and calming vibe. Examples: blue, teal, and turquoise, red, orange, and yellow, green, blue-green, and teal.

Monochromatic: Different shades of the same color offer a classic and elegant look. Examples: navy, royal blue, and light blue, black, charcoal, and grey, brown, beige, and cream.
